Why Golden Batch

Granola Taken Seriously

We obsess over every ingredient and every roast time so you don't have to think twice about breakfast.

Whole-Food Ingredients

Rolled oats, raw nuts, real honey, and cold-pressed oils. No mystery powders, no corn syrup, no "natural flavors" covering up shortcuts.

Baked to Order

Every bag is baked after you order — not months in advance. That means peak crunch, peak flavor, peak freshness every single time.

Rotating Seasonal Flavors

Our bakers craft new flavors every season inspired by local harvests. Your subscription always brings something to look forward to.

Fully Customizable

No Strings Attached
